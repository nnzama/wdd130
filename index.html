South Africa

The Durban South Africa Temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints was announced on October 1, 2011, by President Thomas S. Monson. Ground was broken on April 9, 2016, and the temple was dedicated on February 16, 2020, by Elder Ronald A. Rasband. It is the fifth temple in Africa and the second in South Africa, following the Johannesburg South Africa Temple. The temple serves members in Mozambique, Lesotho, and South Africa.
